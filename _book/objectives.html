3.3 Objectives

Following are listed the main objectives of the project:

  • To explain the COVID-19 pandemic under Air Traffic Network point of view.
  • To generate a database listing all the alterations in the schedules of the passenger service airlines during the period of time selected.
  • To represent the flight adjustments in a clear and attractive way.
  • To explain the COVID-19 pandemic and the measures applied through the schedules adjustments.
  • To analyse the different values collected in order to determine, if present, the strategy followed by the airlines.
  • To generate a interactive document to facilitate the understanding and comprehension of the pandemic outbreak.
